▶ Evidence-Based Nutrition
MACRO
GUIDE
Your Daily Targets
Macro Breakdown
Protein
195g/day · 0.97g per lb bodyweight
Repairs and builds muscle. Spread across all 6 meals (~32g each). Sources: chicken, eggs, whey, salmon, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese.
Carbohydrates
430g/day · 46% of total calories
Primary fuel for heavy training. Front-load carbs pre and post workout. Sources: oats, white rice, sweet potato, quinoa, banana, fruit.
Fats
95g/day · 23% of total calories
Supports testosterone and joint health. Keep lower around training. Sources: salmon, eggs, avocado, olive oil, nuts, peanut butter.
Your Calorie Numbers
TDEE Breakdown
🔥
BMR
~2,020 kcal · calories burned at rest
Mifflin-St Jeor formula: (10×91)+(6.25×180)−(5×25)+5. Based on 200 lbs/91kg, assumed 5'11"/180cm, age 25. Plug your exact height into tdeecalculator.net.
⚡
TDEE (Maintenance)
~3,485 kcal · BMR × 1.725
Calories you burn daily training 6 days/week. Eat at this number = weight stays the same. This is your baseline.
📈
Lean Bulk Target
3,720 kcal · TDEE + ~235 surplus
Small surplus = muscle gain with minimal fat. Target 0.25–0.5 lb/week. Adjust ±150 cal every 2–3 weeks based on results.
Supplement Stack
Evidence-Based Only
💊
Creatine Monohydrate
3–5g daily · Any time of day
Most researched supplement in existence. Increases strength, power, and muscle volume. Non-negotiable for advanced lifters.
🥛
Whey Protein
1–2 scoops · Post-workout + snacks
Fast-absorbing protein. Use to conveniently hit 195g/day. Not required if getting enough from whole food.
☕
Caffeine
150–200mg · 30 min pre-training
Proven to increase strength output and endurance. Black coffee works perfectly — no need for expensive pre-workouts. Cap at 400mg/day.
😴
Casein Protein
Before bed · Optional
Slow-digesting, feeds muscles overnight. Cottage cheese is a perfect natural substitute — equally effective and cheaper.
